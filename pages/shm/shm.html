Simple Harmonic Motion
Intro

The angle for simple harmonic motion with respect to t, can be expressed as:

\[\theta (t) = \theta_{max} \cos\frac{2\pi}{T}t \tag{1}\]

where the time period can be calculated by:

\[T=2\pi \sqrt\frac{L}{g}\tag{2}\]
